about me

I am Patience ISHIMWE, I started software enginnering career in 2019. Over the year I worked with many languages, technologies, and tools like Java, Javascript, Python,React,Html, css....

I work to make a better web; one that is fast, easy to use, beautiful, accessible to all, and frustration-free. Regardless of your specific business requirements, in solving these challenges, you have a great chance of finding success online.I have experience in both front-end and backend development with modern technologies.
